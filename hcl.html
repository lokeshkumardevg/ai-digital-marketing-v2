Overview

Reimagining Enterprise Potential with AI-Intrinsic Transformation

At HCLTech, we see AI not as an overlay, but as core to the next generation of enterprise innovation. Since 2016, we have been at the forefront of AI advancement, from foundational AI and early automation to the breakthroughs of GenAI and now the emergence of Agentic AI.

As industries shift from experimentation to AI at scale, we enable enterprises to embrace AI as a native capability embedded across systems, workflows and decision-making, boosting operational efficiency, enabling entirely new capabilities and opening pathways to new markets.

AI Engineering

Under our AI Engineering suite, we offer two transformative capabilities designed to bridge the physical and digital worlds, enabling intelligent, adaptive and scalable systems:

  • Physical AI Solutions - focused on embedding intelligence into physical systems and environments.
  • Kinetic AI Solutions - centered on robotics and intelligent automation, enabling systems that move, interact and adapt in real-world environments.

Frequently Asked Questions about AI

What is HCLTech’s approach to AI strategy consulting?

At HCLTech, our AI strategy consulting blends deep industry insight with hands-on experience. We help clients cut through the hype, assess real-world opportunities and design tailored AI roadmaps. Our approach focuses on aligning AI initiatives with business goals, ensuring scalable, responsible adoption and measurable value at every stage.

How do you ensure responsible and ethical AI implementation?

Responsible AI is at the core of our methodology. Through our Office of Responsible AI and Governance, we guide clients in ethical AI deployment, emphasizing transparency, fairness and compliance with global standards. We combine technical rigor with robust governance to ensure our AI solutions are trustworthy, explainable and aligned with client values.

How do you use Generative AI and Agentic AI?

We leverage generative AI to automate workflows, enhance creativity and accelerate solution development. With Agentic AI, we enable enterprises to deploy autonomous, self-driven systems that collaborate with human teams. Our expertise helps clients harness these technologies to boost productivity, adaptability and innovation across their organizations.

Can HCLTech's AI solutions be tailored to our industry and needs?

Absolutely. We tailor AI solutions to each client’s industry, business model and unique challenges. Our teams combine sector-specific expertise with flexible delivery models, ensuring every solution—from AI consulting to automation platforms—is designed for maximum relevance, impact and value in your operational context.

How do you protect data and secure AI models?

Data protection and AI security are foundational to our approach. To safeguard client data, we implement rigorous governance, robust encryption and secure model management. Our Responsible AI practices ensure compliance with industry regulations, while our security experts continuously monitor and update defenses to address evolving threats.

What comprehensive AI services do you offer to businesses and enterprises?

We offer a full spectrum of AI services for business, including AI consulting, solution design, systems integration, managed services, Generative AI, Agentic AI and AI automation platforms. Our end-to-end capabilities help enterprises accelerate transformation, improve productivity, modernize IT and unlock new revenue streams across diverse industries.

What tangible business outcomes can we expect from your AI services?

Clients see measurable outcomes from our AI services, including increased operational efficiency, accelerated innovation, improved client loyalty and new revenue opportunities. We focus on delivering ROI by automating processes, optimizing value streams and enabling new capabilities that drive competitive advantage and sustainable business growth.

How do you integrate AI solutions with existing IT infrastructure and ensure scalability?

Our integration approach ensures AI solutions fit seamlessly with your existing IT landscape. We leverage best-of-breed partnerships, cloud AI and hybrid architectures for scalable deployments. Our proven frameworks and engineering mindset help clients modernize infrastructure, address tech debt and scale AI initiatives enterprise-wide with confidence.

What expertise and support do you provide across the AI journey?

We support clients throughout their AI journey—from initial strategy and consulting to solution development, deployment and ongoing optimization. Our global AI Labs, skilled engineering teams and managed services ensure continuous innovation, robust support and rapid scaling, making us a trusted partner for long-term AI success.

Can you share examples of successful AI implementations?

Certainly. We’ve helped clients across industries automate business operations, modernize IT and drive innovation using AI/GenAI. From AI-powered service transformation platforms to agentic enterprise solutions, our implementations consistently deliver measurable efficiency gains, cost reductions and new market opportunities—demonstrating real-world value and lasting impact.
